# Social Media Checklist for Responsible Use (College Students)

## ✅ Do's
- Do think before you post or share any content.
- Do protect your privacy by adjusting account settings.
- Do verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Do maintain a respectful and professional tone in your posts and comments.
- Do use social media for learning, networking, and skill development.

## ❌ Don'ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, passwords).
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or hate speech.
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
- Don’t spend excessive time on social media that affects your studies or health.
