# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

## ✅ Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, and symbols).
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
- Do not click on suspicious links received via email, SMS, or messaging apps.
- Always verify the sender before sharing any personal or sensitive information.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts or making transactions.
- Be cautious while downloading apps—install only from trusted sources like official app stores.
- Log out from shared or public computers after use.

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
- Always double-check the receiver’s UPI ID or QR code before making payments.
- Do not approve “collect requests” from unknown contacts on UPI apps.
- Immediately report any unauthorized transaction to your bank.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for immediate assistance in financial fraud cases)

## ⚠️ Remember
Staying alert and informed is the best way to protect yourself from cyber threats.
